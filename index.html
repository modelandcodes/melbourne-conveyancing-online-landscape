Dominant Players: Website Archetypes in the SERP

The top search results are primarily composed of three distinct types of websites, each playing a different role in guiding users.

1. Individual Conveyancing Firms (50%)

These are specialized firms or legal practices with a strong conveyancing focus, directly offering services. Their goal is client acquisition. Examples: `conveyancing.com.au`, `leadconveyancing.com.au`.

2. Legal Practices with Conveyancing Focus (30%)

The mix indicates that users seeking "top 10" results are well-served by both comparison sites and direct service providers with strong online presences.

Digital Maturity

The Melbourne conveyancing market shows strong adoption of sophisticated digital marketing, transparent pricing, and online service delivery among its top players.
